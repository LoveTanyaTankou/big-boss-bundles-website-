Shipping Policy

            

                This policy explains our processing times, shipping methods,                 delivery responsibilities, tracking procedures, and local                 pickup requirements.

Shipping Summary

                    

                        Products available locally may be eligible for                         pickup after confirmation.                     

                    

                        Orders not immediately available generally require                         approximately 7 to 10 business days for processing                         and fulfillment.                     

                    

                        Local pickup customers must call ahead and wait for                         confirmation that the order is ready.                     

                
                
                    

1. Order Processing Times

                    

                        Processing begins after payment has been successfully                         authorized and the order has been accepted.                     

                    

                        Most orders not immediately available for local pickup                         generally require approximately                         7 to 10 business days                         for processing and fulfillment.                     

                    

                        Processing time is separate from carrier transit time.                         The delivery estimate begins after the carrier accepts                         the shipment.                     

                    

Orders That May Require Additional Time

                    

                        Additional processing time may be required for:                     

                    
                            
  • Extended hair lengths
    •                         
  • High-density wigs
    •                         
  • Special lace sizes
    •                         
  • Customized products
    •                         
  • Large-quantity orders
    •                         
  • Specially sourced hair origins or textures
    •                         
  • Items affected by supplier or carrier delays
    •                         
  • Orders placed during holidays or major promotions
    •                     
                    

                        Business days do not include Saturdays, Sundays,                         federal holidays, or days when Big Boss Bundles or                         applicable carriers are closed.                     

                
                
                    

2. Shipping and Estimated Delivery

                    

                        Delivery estimates shown during checkout are estimates                         and are not guaranteed arrival dates unless Big Boss                         Bundles expressly states otherwise in writing.                     

                    

                        Final delivery timing depends on:                     

                    
                            
  • Order processing time
    •                         
  • Selected shipping method
    •                         
  • Customer location
    •                         
  • Carrier operations
    •                         
  • Weather and emergency conditions
    •                         
  • Holiday shipping volume
    •                         
  • Address accuracy
    •                     
                    

                        Expedited shipping, when offered, speeds up carrier                         transit after processing. It does not necessarily                         shorten product processing or sourcing time.                     

                
                
                    

3. Tracking Information

                    

                        When tracking is available, the customer will receive                         tracking information using the email address,                         telephone number, customer account, or marketplace                         associated with the order.                     

                    

                        Tracking information may take time to update after the                         shipping label is created. A label-created status does                         not always mean that the carrier has physically                         scanned or accepted the package.                     

                    

                        Customers are responsible for monitoring tracking and                         making reasonable arrangements to receive the package.                     

                
                
                    

4. Shipping Address Accuracy

                    

                        Customers are responsible for submitting a complete                         and accurate shipping address before payment.                     

                    

                        Review the following carefully:                     

                    
                            
  • Recipient name
    •                         
  • Street address
    •                         
  • Apartment, suite, or unit number
    •                         
  • City
    •                         
  • State
    •                         
  • ZIP code
    •                         
  • Telephone number
    •                     
                    

                        Address changes are not guaranteed after an order is                         placed. Big Boss Bundles may require identity or                         payment verification before approving an address                         change.                     

                    

                        The customer may be responsible for additional                         shipping costs when a package is returned, delayed,                         or redirected because of an incomplete, incorrect,                         or undeliverable address.                     

                
                
                    

5. Signature Confirmation

                    

                        Big Boss Bundles may require signature confirmation                         on orders to protect the customer and reduce delivery                         fraud.                     

                    

                        The customer is responsible for ensuring that an                         authorized person is available to receive and sign                         for the package when required.                     

                    

                        Carrier instructions, missed-delivery notices,                         redelivery attempts, and pickup deadlines are governed                         by the carrier’s procedures.                     

                
                
                    

6. Unexpected Shipping or Fulfillment Delays

                    

                        We make reasonable efforts to ship orders within the                         time stated on the website, at checkout, or in the                         order confirmation.                     

                    

                        If Big Boss Bundles learns that an order cannot ship                         within the promised period, we may provide a revised                         shipping date and allow the customer to accept the                         delay or cancel the unshipped merchandise as required                         by applicable law.                     

                    

                        If no specific shipping period is stated, applicable                         federal rules generally require online sellers to have                         a reasonable basis to expect shipment within 30 days.                          [oai_citation:1‡Federal Trade Commission](https://www.ftc.gov/legal-library/browse/rules/mail-internet-or-telephone-order-merchandise-rule?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

  • Government restrictions

7. Lost, Missing, or Stolen Packages

                    

                        Contact Big Boss Bundles promptly if tracking shows                         unusual inactivity, delivery to an incorrect location,                         or a package that cannot be located.                     

                    

                        Customers may also need to contact the carrier, submit                         a carrier claim, speak with household members or                         neighbors, check building management, review security                         footage, or contact local authorities when theft is                         suspected.                     

                    

                        A carrier’s delivered scan does not automatically                         establish that Big Boss Bundles caused a package to be                         lost or stolen after delivery.                     

                    

                        We will reasonably assist with available shipment                         information and applicable carrier-claim procedures,                         but replacement or reimbursement is not guaranteed                         when the carrier confirms delivery to the address                         provided by the customer.                     

                
                
                    

8. Packages Damaged During Shipping

                    

                        Photograph the shipping box, label, interior packaging,                         and product before discarding any materials if an order                         arrives visibly damaged.                     

                    

                        Contact Big Boss Bundles within five calendar days                         after receiving the order and provide:                     

                    
                            
  • Customer name
    •                         
  • Order number
    •                         
  • Description of the damage
    •                         
  • Photographs of the packaging and product
    •                         
  • Video when reasonably requested
    •                     
                    

                        Do not install, wear, open, alter, color, wash, or use                         a disputed product while the claim is being reviewed.

10. TikTok Shop, Amazon, and Facebook Orders

                    

                        Purchases made through TikTok Shop, Amazon, Facebook                         Shop, or another marketplace may also be subject to                         that platform’s processing, tracking, delivery,                         cancellation, buyer-protection, and dispute procedures.                     

                    

                        When marketplace requirements conflict with this                         policy, the applicable marketplace rules may control                         the transaction processed through that platform.                     

                
                
                    

11. International Shipping

                    

                        International shipping may be offered only to selected                         destinations and may be unavailable for certain                         products or orders.                     

                    

                        International customers are responsible for customs                         duties, import taxes, brokerage fees, destination                         charges, and local requirements unless checkout                         clearly states otherwise.                     

                    

                        Customs clearance may delay delivery. Big Boss Bundles                         does not control customs agencies or destination-country                         processing.                     

                    

                        Packages refused because of unpaid duties, taxes, or                         customs charges may be subject to return shipping,                         carrier charges, and other costs permitted by law.                     

                
                
                    

12. Policy Updates

                    

                        Big Boss Bundles may update this Shipping Policy as                         carriers, fulfillment procedures, products, sales                         channels, or legal requirements change.                     

                    

                        The policy in effect when the order is submitted                         generally governs that order, subject to applicable                         law and marketplace requirements.

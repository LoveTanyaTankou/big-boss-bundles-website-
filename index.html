CUSTOMER CARE

                

Frequently Asked Questions

            
            
                
                    How long does the hair last?                     

                        With proper care, Big Boss Bundles premium hair can                         last for several years.                     

                
                
                    Can the hair be colored?                     

                        Yes. Our premium raw hair can be professionally colored                         and lifted up to 613 blonde.                     

                
                
                    How should I care for my hair?                     

                        Wash regularly, condition as needed, avoid excessive                         product buildup, and store the hair properly.                     

                
                
                    Can I curl or straighten the hair?                     

                        Yes. The hair can be curled, straightened, and styled.                         We recommend using heat protectant before applying heat.

Frequently Asked Questions

    
        

How long does the hair last?

        

            Our premium raw hair and wigs can last for several years with proper care and maintenance.             Treat the hair as you would your own natural hair by washing, conditioning,             and storing it properly when not in use.         

 
    
        

Can the hair be colored?

        

            Yes. Our premium raw hair can be professionally colored and lifted up to a beautiful             613 blonde while maintaining softness, shine, and quality.         

    
    
        

How should I care for my hair?

        

            Wash regularly, condition as needed, and avoid excessive product buildup.             Heavy oils, creams, and styling products can weigh the hair down.             Less is often more when caring for premium raw hair.         

    
    
        

Can I curl or straighten the hair?

        

            Yes. Our premium raw hair can be curled, straightened, and styled just like             your natural hair. We recommend using a heat protectant when applying heat.
